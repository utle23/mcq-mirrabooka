Best Checklist Compliance Score
A percentage score (0–100%) measuring quality and consistency of checklist completion.
Formula:
(Completion Rate × 45%) +
(On-Time Rate × 25%) +
(Verified Rate × 20%) +
(Clean Rate × 10%) −
min(20, violation penalty × 0.25)
Completion RateTasks ticked ÷ total tasks
On-Time RateSubmitted without late flag
Verified RateManager verified sessions
Clean RateSessions with no issues found
Violation penalty is capped at 20 pts deduction from checklist score.
